GenoCanyon Web Application

We developed a WebApp for GenoCanyon using the shiny package in RStudio. It is designed for analyzing genomic regions of moderate size. The prediction score in the selected region is plotted, showing the functional potential of different segments in this region. You can also choose to highlight a sub-region using a different color. Boxplots and summary statistics are also generated to help decide whether the highlighted sequence is an important segment in this region. The default setting of the WebApp is the HBB gene complex (Chr11: 5225000 - 5320000; hg19).
